About college

The Nitte Mahalinga Adyantaya Memorial Institute of Technology (NMAMIT) is an autonomous engineering college located in the Udupi district of Karnataka. Established in 1986, it is a constituent college of Nitte (Deemed to be University).

NMAMIT is on a 125-acre campus in Nitte. It has academic autonomy to design its curriculum and examination systems. The institution has an "A+" Grade from NAAC and seven undergraduate programs accredited by NBA under the Tier-I category. It was ranked in the 151-200 band for Engineering by NIRF in 2024 and recognized in the "Platinum" category by the AICTE-CII Survey for its industry linkages.
